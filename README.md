## Why NLP + Knowledge Graphs

LLMs alone hallucinate and go stale. Knowledge graphs provide structured, verifiable facts. Retrieval connects them to live information. The systems worth building sit at the intersection of all three — and that's what I focus on.

## The pipeline I build

`PDF / text` → `NER` → `Entity linking` → `Knowledge graph` → `SPARQL` → `LLM + RAG` → `FastAPI service`

## Pinned projects

| Repo | What it does |
|---|---|
| **wikidata-entity-linker** | Dockerized FastAPI service that resolves person names to Wikidata QIDs and answers knowledge questions via SPARQL |
